- `RATES_DURATION`
- `SOX_SEMI`
- `VIX_VXN_REGIME`
- `MEGA_CAP_CONCENTRATION`

- `NQ_PRICE_DISCOVERY`
- `OPEN_GAP_RESIDUAL`
- `KNOWN_FACTOR_MIRAGE`
- `REGIME_SIGN_FLIP`
